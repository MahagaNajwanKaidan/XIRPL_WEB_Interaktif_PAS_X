Gado-gado amazes the tongue with a combination of rich and balanced flavors. Fresh vegetables that are perfectly boiled or marinated are combined with a savory peanut sauce, giving a sweet, savory and slightly spicy sensation that tempts the taste buds. This balanced taste makes every bite a pleasure in itself.

Gado-gado tempts the taste buds with a variety of ingredients used. Tofu, tempeh, potatoes, bean sprouts and lontong create an interesting combination of textures. The softness of the tofu and tempeh combines with the chewiness of the lontong and potatoes, while the bean sprouts provide a refreshing touch of freshness.
